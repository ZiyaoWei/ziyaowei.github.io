CollatzReich

A musical exploration of the Collatz/3n+1 conjecture, inspired by Steve Reich's "Clapping Music".

Enter two starting numbers, or let Randomize choose two numbers for you.

Each number's binary representation becomes a rhythmic pattern, where 1s are claps.

As each number follows the Collatz sequence, its binary pattern evolves until reaching 1.
